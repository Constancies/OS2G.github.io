Meetings

OS2G meets in-person on Fridays from 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM in Avery Hall room 19. Our typical meeting structures include:

  • Talk days, where someone from inside or outside the club give an informative talk.
  • Social days, which are more casual. People work on homework or projects, and discuss current tech events.
  • Fun days, which are scheduled events such as Soup Day, Mana Games day, or movie nights.
